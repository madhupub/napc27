About NAPC-2027

NAPC-2027 is the sixth edition of this prestigious conference, dedicated to advancing the frontiers of aerospace propulsion. It brings together experts, researchers, startups and professionals from the propulsion community to share insights, discuss advancements, and foster collaboration.

Organized by

MAE Department, IIT Hyderabad

Under Auspices of

NCABE & GTRE, Bangalore

About IITH and MAE

The department was envisioned as a Mechanical and Aerospace Department to leverage the strengths of both the disciplines and to support the many Aerospace industries in the Telangana region. Under able and visionary leadership and with the support of both young and experienced faculty members and staff forming its core competency, the department has grown significantly. With a focus on interdisciplinary research and teaching, we are actively involved in unique undergraduate and graduate programs such as B.Tech programs in Computational Mechanics, and Engineering Sciences as well as M.Tech programs in Additive Manufacturing, Smart Mobility, Ophthalmic Engineering, Polymers and Biosystems, Integrated Sensors System, Integrated Circuits and Microsystem Packaging.

With a focus on interdisciplinary research and teaching, we are involved in unique programs such as B.Tech in Computational Mechanics and Engineering Sciences, and M.Tech in Additive Manufacturing, Smart Mobility, and Integrated Sensors.

Conference Scope

The NAPC-2027 serves as a unifying platform for the entire propulsion community, encompassing both air breathing and non-air-breathing propulsion technologies. This conference provides an invaluable opportunity to showcase research and development activities, engage with peers, and cultivate future collaborations through networking.
