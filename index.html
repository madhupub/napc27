NAPC 2027

NAPC 2027 marks the sixth edition of the National Aerospace Propulsion Conference, a flagship biennial event themed ‘Advancements in Propulsion Systems for Next-Gen Aerospace Vehicles’. This prestigious gathering explores cutting-edge innovations in sustainable, high-performance propulsion for aircraft, drones, eVTOLs, hypersonics, and reusable rockets.

NCABE & NAPC

The National Council for Air Breathing Engines was founded in 1992 as a trust to provide a forum and stimulus to R&D activities in aerospace propulsion in India. The trust held the first National Conference on Air-Breathing Engines (NCABE) in Bangalore in 1992, and since then, NCABE has been a major technical event hosted across premier aerospace institutions.

NCABE serves as the Indian complement to the International Conference on Air Breathing Engines (ISABE). The National Aerospace Propulsion Conference (NAPC) is a national-level conference dedicated to propulsion technologies, formed by merging the National Propulsion Conference (NPC) and NCABE.

This biennial conference brings together the entire propulsion ecosystem—industry, academia, and research laboratories—providing a platform to showcase research, exchange ideas, and foster collaborations.

About IITH and MAE

The Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering was established to unify the strengths of both fields and advance innovation in science and technology. Driven by visionary leadership and a dedicated team, the department excels in interdisciplinary research, particularly in combustion and propulsion, supported by specialized laboratories and collaborative faculty.

Submission Guidelines

Authors are requested to follow the guidelines below while preparing and submitting their manuscripts.

General Guidelines

  • The extended abstract should not exceed two pages.
  • The full paper should not exceed ten pages.
  • The extended abstract must include all sections of the full paper such as Introduction, Method/Experimental Setup, Results, and Conclusion.
  • All submissions must be original and should not have been published previously or be under consideration elsewhere.
  • Authors must ensure proper formatting as per the conference template.

Submission Checklist

  • The manuscript complies with the submission template and formatting requirements.
  • All authors' details and affiliations are correctly mentioned.
  • Figures and tables are properly labeled and cited in the text.
  • References are formatted according to the prescribed style.
  • The submission file is in PDF format.

Important Note

Submissions that do not adhere to the above guidelines may be returned to the authors for revision before being considered for review.
